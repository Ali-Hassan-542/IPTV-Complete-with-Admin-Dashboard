Frequently Asked Questions

What is IPTV?
IPTV (Internet Protocol Television) is a service that provides television programming and other video content using the Internet Protocol (IP) instead of traditional cable or satellite formats. It allows you to stream media content in real-time or on-demand over the internet.
Do I need a VPN?
While StreamZone works without a VPN in most regions, we recommend using a VPN for enhanced privacy and to access geo-restricted content. A VPN can also help stabilize your connection in some cases.
Can I watch on multiple devices?
Yes! Depending on your subscription plan, you can watch on multiple devices simultaneously. Our Basic plan allows 1 connection, Standard allows 2, and Premium allows 4 connections at the same time.
What happens if my subscription expires?
If your subscription expires, you will lose access to the service. We send renewal reminders via email before your subscription ends. You can renew at any time to continue enjoying our service.
How do I contact support?
You can contact our 24/7 support team through the contact form on our website, via email at support@streamzone.com, or through our WhatsApp and Telegram support channels listed in the contact section.
