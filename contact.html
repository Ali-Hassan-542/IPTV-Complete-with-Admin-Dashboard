Contact FAQ

Quick answers to common questions about contacting our support team.

What is the average response time for support requests?
We strive to respond to all support requests as quickly as possible. For WhatsApp and Telegram, responses are typically within 15-30 minutes during business hours. Email inquiries are answered within 2-4 hours. Live chat support provides instant responses during operating hours.
What information should I include when contacting support?
To help us resolve your issue quickly, please include: your account email, subscription plan, device type, app version (if applicable), and a detailed description of the issue you're experiencing. If possible, include screenshots or error messages.
What are your support hours?
Our support team is available 24/7 through WhatsApp and Telegram. Email support is monitored 24/7 with responses sent during business hours. Live chat is available from 9:00 AM to 11:00 PM EST, 7 days a week.
Do you provide support in languages other than English?
Yes! Our support team includes multilingual agents who can assist you in Spanish, French, Arabic, and German. Please indicate your preferred language when contacting us, and we'll connect you with an appropriate team member.
