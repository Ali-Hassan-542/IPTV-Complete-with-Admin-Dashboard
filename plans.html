Plan FAQ

Find answers to common questions about our IPTV subscription plans and billing.

Can I change my plan after subscribing?
Yes, you can upgrade your plan at any time. The new plan will be effective immediately, and you'll be charged the prorated difference for the remainder of your billing cycle. Downgrades will take effect at the start of your next billing cycle.
Do you offer refunds?
We offer a 7-day money-back guarantee for all new subscriptions. If you're not satisfied with our service within the first 7 days, you can request a full refund. After 7 days, refunds are issued on a case-by-case basis for technical issues we cannot resolve.
How many devices can I use simultaneously?
The number of simultaneous connections depends on your plan. Basic allows 1 connection, Standard allows 2, Family allows 3, Premium allows 4, and Ultimate allows 5 connections. You can use these connections on any compatible devices.
Is there a contract or commitment?
No, there are no contracts or long-term commitments. All our plans are month-to-month (or for the duration you select). You can cancel at any time, and your service will remain active until the end of your paid period.
Do you offer discounts for long-term subscriptions?
Yes, we offer significant discounts for longer subscription periods. For example, our Premium plan offers 42% savings when you subscribe for 12 months compared to the monthly rate. The Ultimate plan offers 50% savings for a 24-month subscription.
